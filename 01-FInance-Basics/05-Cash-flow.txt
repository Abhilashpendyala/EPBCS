Cash flow statement :

cash flow = cash inflows - cash outflows

explains difference between cash between two balance sheets

closing balance of previous year is opening balance of this year

cash flow statement derives insights in understanding what caused cash change

methods of making cash flow statements

start with net profit, add back non cash expenses like depreciation and amortization , changes in working capital and record financing activities that has dealt with cash  --> indirect methods

start from scratch: from opening cash balance add cash expenses receipt by receipt --> direct method
